<p><p>The GPS Auto Monitoring System market features various transmission types, including Wired Transmission Systems and Dial Transmission Mode Systems. Wired Transmission Systems utilize physical cables to transmit real-time data from vehicles to monitoring centers, ensuring stable and secure communication. In contrast, Dial Transmission Mode Systems use telephone lines to relay information, often employing periodic data updates. Both systems enable effective tracking and management of vehicle fleets, enhancing operational efficiency and security in transportation industries.</p></p>
