<p><p>The industrial separation and sifting equipment market includes various types designed for efficient material processing. Vibratory type equipment uses controlled vibrations to move and separate materials based on size. Ultrasonic type employs high-frequency sound waves, enhancing separation efficiency for fine particles and difficult materials. Airstream type utilizes airflow to separate lighter particles from heavier ones, often used for dust and debris removal. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, enhancing productivity and ensuring quality in material processing.</p></p>
