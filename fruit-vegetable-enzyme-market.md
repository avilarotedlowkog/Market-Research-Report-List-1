Pectinase is utilized for enhancing juice extraction and improving clarity in fruit processing. Amylase aids in breaking down starches into sugars, often used in brewing and baking. Protease facilitates protein digestion, beneficial in tenderizing meat and vegan product development. Cellulase helps in breaking down cellulose, enhancing the extraction of juice and improving texture in processed fruits and vegetables. These enzymes play vital roles across food industries.</p></p>
