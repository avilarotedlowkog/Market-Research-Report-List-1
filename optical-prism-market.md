<p><p>The optical prism market includes various types tailored for specific applications. Right angle prisms are used for light deflection, while diamond prisms offer high light transmission and minimal distortion. Pentagonal prisms are designed for image rotation, and total internal reflection (TIR) prisms enhance light efficiency. Cube beamsplitters divide light into multiple paths, and roof prisms invert images, commonly used in binoculars. Other prisms encompass specialty designs for niche applications, expanding the versatility of optical technologies.</p></p>
