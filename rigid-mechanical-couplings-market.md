<p><ul><li>Sleeve</li><li>Flanged</li><li>Clamped</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rigid mechanical couplings market comprises three main types: sleeve couplings, flanged couplings, and clamped couplings. Sleeve couplings connect two shafts using a cylindrical sleeve, providing a simple and effective alignment solution. Flanged couplings feature two flanges that bolt together, offering robust connections for high torque applications. Clamped couplings use a clamping mechanism for easy installation and removal, allowing for minor adjustments in shaft alignment. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing performance and reliability in various mechanical systems.</p></p>
