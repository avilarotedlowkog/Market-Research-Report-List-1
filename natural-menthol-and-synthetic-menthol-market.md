<p><p>Natural menthol and synthetic menthol are two forms of menthol, a compound widely recognized for its cooling and soothing properties. Both types are employed across various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, cosmetics, and personal care products.</p><p>The Natural Menthol and Synthetic Menthol Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.8% during the forecast period. Additionally, the rise of the wellness and personal care sectors is propelling the market further, with natural menthol gaining traction due to its perceived health benefits. Emerging trends include the development of sustainable extraction techniques for natural menthol and innovations in synthetic menthol production, aimed at enhancing purity and cost-efficiency.
