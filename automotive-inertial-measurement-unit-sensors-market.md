<p><strong>The Automotive Inertial Measurement Unit Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs</li><li>FOG-based IMUs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive inertial measurement unit (IMU) sensors market is primarily divided into MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs and FOG-based IMUs. MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs utilize micro-electromechanical systems to provide precise motion tracking, offering benefits like compact size and low power consumption, making them suitable for most automotive applications. In contrast, FOG-based IMUs leverage fiber optic technology for higher accuracy and stability, ideal for high-performance vehicles and applications requiring robust navigation capabilities, such as autonomous driving and advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
