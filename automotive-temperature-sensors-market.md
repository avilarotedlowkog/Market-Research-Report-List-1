<p><strong>The Automotive Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermistor</li><li>Resistance Temperature Detector</li><li>Thermocouple</li><li>IC Temperature Sensor</li><li>MEMS Temperature Sensor</li><li>Infrared Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive temperature sensors market includes several types, each serving specific applications. Thermistors provide high sensitivity for monitoring fluid temperatures. Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs) offer accuracy in measuring engine and exhaust temperatures. Thermocouples are robust sensors suited for extreme conditions. Integrated Circuit (IC) temperature sensors combine circuitry for precise readings in compact spaces. MEMS temperature sensors are miniaturized devices ideal for modern automotive systems. Infrared sensors enable non-contact temperature measurement, enhancing safety and efficiency in vehicle operations.</p></p>
