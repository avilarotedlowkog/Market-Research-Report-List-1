<p><p>Polypropylene woven fabrics are categorized into laminated and unlaminated markets based on their manufacturing processes and end uses. Laminated polypropylene fabrics feature a protective layer, enhancing their durability, moisture resistance, and strength, making them suitable for industrial packaging, agricultural covers, and construction. On the other hand, unlaminated polypropylene fabrics are more breathable and lightweight, commonly utilized in products like shopping bags, geotextiles, and upholstery. Each type caters to specific applications, meeting varying performance and environmental requirements.</p></p>
