<p><ul><li>Single-Spark</li><li>Multi-Spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car ignition coil market is divided into two main types: single-spark and multi-spark ignition coils. Single-spark coils deliver a single high voltage pulse to ignite the air-fuel mixture, suitable for most traditional engines. In contrast, multi-spark coils generate multiple sparks during a combustion cycle, enhancing ignition efficiency and performance, particularly in high-performance or modified engines. Both types cater to different vehicle applications, significantly impacting fuel efficiency, power output, and emissions in modern automotive systems.</p></p>
