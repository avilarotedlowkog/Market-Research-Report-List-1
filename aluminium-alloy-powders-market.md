Medium Aluminium Alloy Powders strike a balance between strength and workability, making them suitable for general-purpose applications. Coarse Aluminium Alloy Powders, characterized by larger particles, are typically utilized in applications where bulk and structural integrity are prioritized, such as in construction and automotive components.</p></p>
